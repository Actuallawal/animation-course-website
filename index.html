Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What are the technical requirements to run Adobe Animate for this course?

A: You'll need:

  • Windows 10 (64-bit) or macOS 10.14+
  • 4GB RAM minimum (8GB or higher recommended)
  • Reasonable available storage

We'll provide installation guidance and optimization tips in the class.

Q: Do I need to purchase Adobe Animate separately?

A: Our students receive a licensed copy of Adobe Animate..

Q: I'm completely new to animation. Will I be able to keep up?

A: Absolutely! The course begins with fundamental animation principles before touching software. We cover:

  • Animation basics (squash/stretch, anticipation, etc.)
  • Adobe Animate interface deep dive
  • Creating your first character rig
  • Basic walk cycles and movements

By week 5, complete beginners are creating fluid animations.

Q: What kinds of animations will we create in the course?

A: You'll complete 5 portfolio-ready projects:

  1. Basic motion graphics (logos/shapes)
  2. Character emotion expressions
  3. Lip-synced dialogue scene
  4. Full character walk/run cycle
  5. 30-second narrative animation

Plus bonus modules on social media formats (TikTok/Reels).

Q: Will you teach professional animation workflows?

A: Yes! We cover industry-standard practices including:

  • Proper layer organization
  • Symbol and component reuse
  • Scene planning with animatics
  • Efficient tweening techniques
  • Export settings for various platforms
  • Collaboration with voice actors

Q: How much time should I dedicate weekly?

A: Recommended weekly commitment:

  • Beginners: 8-10 hours (2hrs video, 6-8hrs practice)
  • Intermediate: 5-7 hours (1.5hrs video, 4-6hrs practice)

The course is designed for 12 weeks but can be completed faster. All content remains accessible forever.

Q: What support is available when I get stuck?

  • 1-on-1 coaching sessions (additional for Starter)
